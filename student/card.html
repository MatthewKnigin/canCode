Card Workshop

Here are some challenges for students to make their cards even more fun!

Challenge 1

Say something!

Make a character say something when you press the space key.

Hint: Add a character from the bottom right, and Use the Say block from Looks

Challenge 2

Celebrate!

When you open your card make your character change color!

Hint: Use the Change Color Effect block from Looks on a certian backdrop...

Challenge 3

Spin!

When you open your card make your character spin! If you're still stuck on the event, try the When Backdrop Switches To (from events)

Hint: Use the Rotate block from Motion

Challenge 4

Transform!

When you open your card make your character change into something else!

Hint: Use the Switch Costume block from Looks

Challenge 5

Introducing...

Add a starting block to your character and make your character tell a story or introduce your card when the Green Flag is Clicked.

Challenge 3

Sounds!

Instead of speech bubbles add in sounds!

Hint: Switch out a Say block for a Play Sound Until Done block from Sound

Challenge 6

Soundtracks!

Have a cool soundtrack playing in the background of your card!

Hint: Use the Play Sound Until Done Block from Sounds
