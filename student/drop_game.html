Drop Game

Here are some challenges for students to make their drop games even more fun!

Workshop 1

Challenge 1

Make Fun Sounds!

When you score a point, have your character make a fun sound of your choice!

Hint: Use the Sound block from Sounds

Challenge 2

10 Point Highlight!

Score 10 points is a big deal! Make a celebratory sound when that milestone is reached.

Hint: Use the Mod block from Operators

Challenge 3

Change Costumes!

Evertime a point is scored, your character changes into a new costume!

Hint: While you will need the Switch Costume block from Looks, the Pick Random block from operators can be useful as well.

Challenge 4

Changing Backgrounds!

Make the background of your game change when a certain event takes place, such as scoring 10 points!

Hint: Use the Next Backdrop block in Looks

Workshop 2

Challenge 1

Rainbow!

Have your dropping sprite change into pretty colors as they fall!

Hint: Use the Change Color Effect By block from Looks

Challenge 2

Soundtracks!

Have a cool soundtrack playing in the background of your game!

Hint: Use the Play Sound Until Done Block from Sounds

Challenge 3

Challenge 4

Start A Streak!

In a similar fashion to how the score count works, create a streak counter!

Hint: Make sure to make a variable and name it Streak

Challenge 5

Difficulty!

Up the difficulty after getting 10 points in a row by increasing the speed your sprite falls!

Hint: Using the Speech variable use the Change Variable By Block
