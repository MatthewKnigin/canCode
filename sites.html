Where do we Teach?

Boys & Girls Clubs


In 2019, we taught a Winter Camp at the Boys & Girls Clubs location in Palm Beach County. Since then we have been continuing to teach there!

Florence Fuller East and West


In 2018, we started teaching at the Western location of Florence Fuller Child Development Centers (FFCDC). We loved it so much that we wanted to teach more kids there! We expanded to FFCDC East, where the staff had already heard about us from FFCDC West.

Adopt a Family


As a part of our 2020 workshop expansion, we began serving elementary schoolers as a part of the Adopt a Family’s Project Grow summer program. Adopt a Family’s Project Grow gives formerly homeless children and foster children free child care and resources.

Pearl CATS


We started teaching at Pearl CATS in 2019. We continue to have programs there including for the 2021 summer!

SOS Foster Village


To kick off 2020, canCode expanded into SOS’s Foster Village in Coconut Creek. The children at SOS absolutely love the canCode workshops. We are so happy to teach there and continue to expand our impact out of Boca Raton.

Public Library


canCode workshops are available to the general public! With our partnership with the Palm Beach County Library System, canCode has been offered online in the 2020-2021 school year. We plan to contine to teach there for the upcoming school year too! Visit the county library’s website to learn more and sign up for canCode workshops.
