Where do we Teach?

The Youth Activity Center

Partner Site


We have been teaching at our first site, the Youth Activity Center (YAC), since Noah started in 2016. Other teachers have taught there as well and we continue to have a close relationship with the site to this day.

Florence Fuller East and West

New Sites!


In 2018, we started teaching at the Western location of Florence Fuller Child Development Centers (FFCDC). We loved it so much that we wanted to teach more kids there! We expanded to FFCDC East, where the staff had already heard about us from FFCDC West.

Boys & Girls Clubs
