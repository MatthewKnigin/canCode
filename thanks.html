Thank you cards for firemen

Dear Firefighters,

We ran 2 extra workshops today to teach the 5th graders at the Achievement Center in Delray how to use their new computer programming skills to code thank you cards for you. We are all so grateful for all that you do in our community. Below are links to the students cards; to see the card click the link and press the green flag, then press the spacebar to open the card up. We hope you enjoy!

We hope to send you more cards in the future. Thank you again for your bravery and help throughout the Delray Beach area.

Thank you,
Noah Rubin
canCode Founder
