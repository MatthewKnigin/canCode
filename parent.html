Parent Resources

Who are we?

canCode is a small group of high school teachers committed to teaching your children how to code. We make learning to code an educational, enriching, and entertaining experience for your children.

What do we teach?

We use Scratch, a fun and interactive free online coding environment developed by MIT. Our first project will teach how to create a maze game from “scratch” in Scratch. Students will design their own individual mazes and fun characters.

Under the Student section of this website, we have put together a set of interesting challenges for students who would like to try coding on their own.

How can I enroll my child?

We teach at the Youth Activity Center (YAC) in Boca Raton, Florida. You do not have to buy any supplies for your child: the YAC already has computers that they have offered to let us use. We usually meet at the YAC on Mondays from about 4 PM to 5 PM.
