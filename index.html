About Us

canCode uses Scratch, a free online coding environment developed by MIT, with engaging and exciting lessons that allow children to create their own unique video games and projects. Teenagers, proficient in coding, create the best possible experience for young children learning to code.

Mission Statement

canCode's mission is to spark an interest in Computer Science in all young children.
