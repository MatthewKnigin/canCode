About canCode

canCode is a group of high school students committed to helping young children learn computer programming. We use Scratch, a fun and interactive free online coding environment developed by MIT.

Why is learning to code important?
Computers already influence almost every area of our lives, from cars to smartphones to video games. Knowing how to code lets you give the instructions that computers need for all these technologies and so much more.

Mission

canCode's mission is to spark an interest in Computer Science and to make coding fun.
