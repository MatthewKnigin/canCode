04:00 PM Eastern Time

Instructions

  1. Click here to join canCode Online Classes. You may choose to download zoom or simply join from your browser.

  2. Press “allow microphone and camera”

  3. Click here to go to the scratch website

  4. Click the “Sign In” button

  5. Enter your username and password. If you don't know your username and password, email us at info@cancode.us with your full name and what center you go to.

  6. Click the folder (not the envelope)

  7. Click on your game

  8. Click “see inside” and you're ready to start learning!
